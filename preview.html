Assumptions (fixed)
  • Property & Tax
  • Prop 13 property tax: 1.25% of purchase price, capped at +2%/yr
  • Insurance: 0.5% of current property value/yr
  • Maintenance: 1% of current property value/yr
  • Depreciation (rental only): IRS 27.5-yr schedule; improvement % adjustable above. Primary residences are not eligible for depreciation deductions.
  • Tax rate: 44% (35% federal + 9.3% CA) on paper rental gain/loss
  • Capital & Returns
  • Initial capital: $100K for all scenarios
  • Wealth = Current Equity + Cumulative Net Cash Flows
  • Reinvested mode: positive cash flows compound at S&P 500 monthly returns; deficit periods accumulate additively (treated as out-of-pocket cost, not leveraged against the market)
  • Note: for highly leveraged scenarios (e.g. 20% down with high mortgage rates), net cash flows are often negative throughout — Reinvested and Additive modes will produce identical results since there is no positive pool to compound
  • Additive mode: dividends & rent cash flows accumulate without compounding
  • Refinance
  • Refi count (0–3): number of times the borrower refinances during the holding period
  • Rule: the model selects the X years with the lowest 30-yr fixed mortgage rates within the chosen period (excluding the purchase year), and refinances at the start of each of those years — but only if the new rate is lower than the current rate
  • Each refinance resets to a new 30-yr amortization on the remaining principal at the new rate; no closing costs modeled
  • Source: FRED MORTGAGE30US annual averages
  • Rent Data
  • BLS West CPI-Rent (1994–2014), Zillow ZORI CA (2015+); pre-1994 estimated from BLS national CPI-Rent series and academic housing literature — less granular, treat as approximate
Sources
